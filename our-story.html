HOW IT

STARTED

CHAPTER 1

THE DORM ROOM

It started in a cramped hostel room at SRM Institute. We were broke college students, but we had standards. We were tired of the harsh ash, the chemical aftertaste, and papers that burned out way before the conversation did.

Between cramming for exams and late-night campus walks, we knew there had to be a better way to roll. So, what do engineers do? They experiment.

CHAPTER 2

THE MASTERPIECE

We didn't want just another paper. We wanted an ultra-thin, 100% organic, unbleached hemp masterpiece. It took months of sourcing, failing, and testing (the fun part), but eventually, we found the perfect slow-burn formula.

CHAPTER 3

THE MOVEMENT

What started as a personal stash for our squad quickly spread like wildfire across the SRM campus. People started asking for "that gang paper."

Now, we're not just a college hustle. GANG is a lifestyle for everyone who respects the ritual, appreciates pure flavor, and stays trippy. No cap.
